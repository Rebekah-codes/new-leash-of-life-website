About Us

New Leash of Life is a small UK pet shop built around practical essentials, good value, and products we’d happily use for our own pets.

Our story

Started for everyday pet needs

The shop began with a simple idea: make it easier to find useful pet products without having to bounce between dozens of sites.

Focused on trusted brands

We keep the range tight and practical, with brands and products chosen for everyday use, comfort, and reliability.

Built for quick shopping

Filters, collections, and clear product pages help you find the right item fast, whether you’re buying treats or replacement essentials.

What we care about

Quality

Clarity

Care

We try to keep the experience friendly, simple, and reassuring.

Freshness

New lines and seasonal picks keep the shop useful through the year.
