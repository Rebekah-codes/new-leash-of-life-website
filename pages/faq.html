Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers to the questions we hear most often. If you still need help, reach out through our contact page.

Orders & delivery

How fast do orders arrive?

Most UK orders are dispatched within 1 to 2 working days and delivered shortly after.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes. Orders over £39 qualify for free delivery across the UK.

Can I track my parcel?

Tracking details are shared when your order leaves the warehouse, so you can follow progress end to end.

Products & returns

How do I choose the right product?

Use the shop filters for pet type, category, brand, or collection to narrow down the right fit quickly.

What if something needs to come back?

Unopened items can be returned in line with our returns policy. Please contact us before sending anything back.

Do you stock new lines regularly?

New products are added throughout the season, so the shop and home page are updated often with fresh picks.
